Off-campus Access

Databases

Our Databases are restricted to current students, faculty, and staff of Bennington College. When you click on a title of a database or electronic resource from off-campus you will be redirected to an authentication page. If you are unsure whether your patron record is current, please feel free to contact us. Resources that are not available off-campus are indicated in the description like so: **Not accessible off campus**.

Steps to Access:

When you click on a resource with restricted access, a authentication page will appear, prompting you to enter the following information:

Your name: Last Name, First Name
Your barcode: 12345

Once you have entered this information click the submit button. You will be redirected to the resource.

Problems with Access

If you are unable to access resources because your patron information is not being accepted, please contact the library. Have someone open your patron account to verify the spelling of your name and your barcode number.

If you are experiencing problems try closing out of the resource and re-entering. Problems with access result either from the long distance sessions timing out or as the result of all of our access licenses for a particular resource being in use at that moment.

If you continue to have difficulties, please email Jared Della Rocca, JDellaRocca@bennington.edu. Please include a detailed account of the steps that you have taken, the wording of the error messages that was generated, and if possible- whether the error messages were generated from the resource website, or the library website.
