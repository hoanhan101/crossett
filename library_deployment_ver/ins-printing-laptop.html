Mac Instructions

  1. Click on the blue apple at the top-left of the screen.
  2. Select System Preferences from the drop down menu.
  3. A window will open with several rows of options. Click on the Printers & Scanners option on the second row.
  4. The window will shift to a layout dominated by two large columns; the left one contains names of printers. Underneath this column are two buttons. One is labeled with a plus sign; click there.
  5. A new window will open. At the top are four options: Default, Fax, IP, and Windows. Select IP Printer.
  6. In the line labeled address, enter the following numbers and periods for the printer of your choice:
    • Enter this for LETHEM 10.10.103.166
    • Enter this for TARTT 10.10.103.167
  7. Once the add button at the bottom of the window is no longer greyed out, click on it. Click "Duplex Printing Unit" on the following screen, then click OK.

Windows Instructions

  1. In the Start Menu, click on Devices and Printers.
  2. This will open another window. On the top click Add a Printer.
  3. A set up wizard will start. Click Add a local printer.
  4. Click on the button to create a new port, accompanied by a drop down menu for you to select the kind of new port. Select Standard TCP/IP Port. Then click Next.
  5. A box will pop up asking for the address of the printer. Enter the following IP address for the printer of your choice and click Next :
    • Enter this for LETHEM 10.10.103.166
    • Enter this for TARTT 10.10.103.167
  6. Then click Next.
  7. Change the printer name according to whichever printer you chose and click Next.
