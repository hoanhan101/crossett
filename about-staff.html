Staff

People in the Library

We all work to make Crossett and Jennings Library the best resource for the extended community of students, staff, faculty, and the broader public. Whether you're interested in making an appointment to meet with a librarian for research help, have a LibGuide created for your course, or want a book added to the library, or any of a myriad of other services we provide, you can find the person responsible below. 

But if you're not sure about who to ask, ask any of the staff or student staff and we can help! The only question we can't answer is the one that goes unasked.

Oceana Wilson

Dean of the Library

Oceana oversees all of the library's services. Ask her questions regarding the library staff, collection, archives budget, and building.

owilson@bennington.edu

Joe Tucker

Research Services Librarian

Joe handles research services for the library. He can help you figure out which resources to use and how to use them best. Faculty may contact him to set up
Information Literacy sessions or LibGuides for a class.

jtucker@bennington.edu

Jared Della Rocca

Outreach Librarian

Jared handles outreach to students through various projects, such as Trivia Night and Librarian Reads Everything, while also assisting with research. He's responsible for the library's social media, and happy to talk books with you all day long.

jdellarocca@bennington.edu

Vanessa Haverkoch

Collection Services Librarian

If it's in the collection, Vanessa had a hand in it. Responsible for cataloging and organizing the collection, and ensuring all our records are correct, up-to-date, and useful in finding the resources patrons need.

vhaver@bennington.edu

Laura Payne

Public Services Coordinator

If nobody else does it, Laura does. Having a hand in budgeting, scheduling, supervising our student staff, and digitizing the college archives, Laura's the person to ask when you're not sure who to ask.

lpayne@bennington.edu

Wade Simpson

Acquisitions Coordinator

If you want it in Crossett, and we don't already own it, he is the person to see. Wade handles the purchasing of books and other materials for the library, tracking orders, prepping
books for the shelves, and any book repairs.

wadesimpson@bennington.edu

Susan Reiss

Jennings Music Library

If it makes music, teaches you to make music, or helps you make music, Susie's in charge
of getting it into
Jennings Music Library.  

susanr@bennington.edu

Kathy Williams

Interlibrary Loan Coordinator

Kathy ensures that the resources available to you extend beyond Crossett Library. If you need an article, book, or DVD from another library, Kathy will get it via Interlibrary Loan through no cost to you. Additionally, she sets up and maintains course reserves.

kathyw@bennington.edu
