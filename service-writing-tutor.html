Writing Tutors


Writing help is available from peer tutors at no charge to all students throughout the week. Students may either make an appointment by stopping by the writing tutor desk in the Reading Room on the second floor of the library during tutoring hours or just walk in for help. Student tutors work with students at all levels and in all disciplines. They can help review grammar, plan research, write outlines, understand essay structures, cite correctly, develop analytical writing, and revise work.
