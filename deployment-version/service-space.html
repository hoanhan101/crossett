Library Spaces


Faculty and students can reserve the library meeting room for informal gatherings, study groups, or meetings. The library meeting room is beautiful space on the top level of the library. It is equipped with digital projection for laptops, DVDs (both domestic and foreign format) and VHS. The space is ideal for 12-15 people.

  • If you would like to teach a class in the library please contact Rosie O'Dell x4405 ROdell@bennington.edu.
  • If you would like to schedule a meeting or gathering in the library please contact Laura Payne LPayne@bennington.edu x4610.

Faculty and students may display art or projects in the library. There are several exhibition space possibilities. If you would like to exhibit your work or work from a course you are teaching please contact Laura Payne LPayne@bennington.edu x4610.
