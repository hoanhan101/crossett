Betsy Sherman Reads the Classics

While working at the front desk, Gabriele Caras overhead Betsy Sherman's plot summaries of books in the display case and thought they were more helpful than any back cover she'd ever read! Of course we couldn't resist collecting some and sharing them with you...

  • Persuasion - Darcy is much cuter than Wentworth
  • Great Expectations - Pip needs to grow a set
  • Moby Dick - I cheered for the whale
  • The Metamorphosis - A guy turns into a bug. Right. I'm sure it's a metaphor
  • Pride and Prejudice - Best book ever. Written in third person, past tense, with a happy ending
  • Revolutionary Suicide - Huey Newton? Didn't he have a rock group in the 80s, or was that Huey Lewis?
  • Lady Chatterley's Lover - I hid this from my mother when I was a teenager
  • Scarlet Letter - Not enough sex
