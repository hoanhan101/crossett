Web Resources

French newspapers online

  • Le Figaro -- French language coverage of political, economic, and social news in France and throughout the world.
  • Le Monde -- Online edition featuring news and information for the francophone world.
  • L'Express -- France's first weekly news magazine.<

Web Sites

  • Lexilogos: Mots et Merveilles des Langues d'ici et d'ailleurs -- Dictionnaire en ligne dans toutes les langues.
  • Fast and Friendly French for Fun -- Nine lessons for beginners, including letters and numbers, subject pronouns, basic verbs and conjugations in the past, present, and future tenses, adjectives, useful phrases, etc.
  • BBC's French Language Homepage -- Includes "French steps" (an online course for beginners) and several intermediate-level listening activities, such as slang, transcripts of a travel documentary, and more.
  • Advocacy for French Language and Culture -- A large collection of web links relating to French language and culture with an emphasis on advocacy for the teaching and learning of French.
  • Francophone Countries -- A list of countries where French is the official language and member states of Organisation Internationale de la Francophonie.
  • Festival International de Louisiane -- Festival International de Louisiane is the United States' largest Francophone music festival, showcasing dozens of artists from around the globe every year.
