Step 2: Senior Project Submission Instructions

Upload via Google Drive

Step 3: Go to Google Drive.

Step 4: Click "NEW" on the left side, and choose Folder. Name the Folder, "CAPA, LastName, FirstName, ClassYear", filling in your particular name and class year. For example, "CAPA, Della Rocca, Jared, 2016"

Step 5: Open up that folder on Google Drive. Click "NEW" on the left side, and choose "File Upload." Navigate to and choose your senior project files, and click Open.

Step 6: When the files are done uploading, click the "Share" button on the top of the screen, make sure you are sharing the ENTIRE FOLDER, share with vhaver@bennington.edu.

Step 7: Pat yourself on the back. You have officially submitted your senior project to Crossett Library!
