In order to better serve the Bennington College community, Crossett Library Interlibrary Loan has transitioned to a new cloud-based Interlibrary Loan platform that replaces ILLiad.

Those interested in using interlibrary loan to borrow items from other libraries should create a new account, even if you've used ILLiad in the past.

You must use your Bennington College email address when you register for your Interlibrary Loan account. We will contact you regarding your requests through this email.

Click here: Interlibrary Loan to log in or to create your account. First-time users should click the gray box: "Don't have an Account? Create Account". Passwords are case-sensitive and must be at least six characters or more, with at least one non-alphabetical character.
