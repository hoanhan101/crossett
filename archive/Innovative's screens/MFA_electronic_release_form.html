Step 1: Complete Digital Archive Deposit License on the left.
*We cannot accept your thesis submission until the license has been completed.

Step 2: Thesis Submission Instructions

Step 3: Go to Google Drive.

Step 4: Click "NEW" on the left side, and choose "File Upload." Navigate to and choose your thesis file, and click Open.

Step 5: Rename your file "LastName, FirstName, ClassYear", filling in your particular name and class year. For example, "Della Rocca, Jared, 2016"

Step 6: Choose the file and click the "Share" button on the top of the screen, share with vhaver@bennington.edu.

Step 7: Pat yourself on the back. You have officially submitted your thesis to Crossett Library!
