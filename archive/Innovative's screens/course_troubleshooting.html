Course Reserves Troubleshooting

Please review the following options to find solutions.

I am getting a message telling me I do not have permission to access my course reserves.

You should contact the library staff and have us verify your name and barcode information is entered correctly in our system. If you have recently gotten a new ID card or have never used the library before, we may not have your barcode in our system. Please call 440-4600 or email the library.

I login to view my course reserves document, and all I get is a white screen.

Try setting your browser to download PDF documents to your desktop and open them in Adobe Reader, or another program that will open PDF files. You can download a free copy of Adobe Reader here.

How to set your Firefox Browser to download PDFs to your desktop:

  • In your open Firefox browser, click on File, Options. (If you are using a Mac, it is Firefox, Preferences.)
  • Click on the Content tab.
  • Towards the bottom should be a section labeled 'File Types'. Click on the Manage button under this heading.
  • Locate in the list 'PDF Adobe Acrobat Document'. Select it, and click Change Action.
  • Select the first option "Open with the Default Application". Click OK.
  • Close the window, and click OK to close the Preferences window.
