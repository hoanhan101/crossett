Naxos Music Library Cookie Settings

Below are detailed instructions for making sure that your cookie handling options are compatible with Naxos Music Library:

Mac Internet Explorer 5.0 or higher

From the main menu at the top of the screen, choose "EDIT", then "PREFERENCES". Under the "Receiving Files" category, go to "Cookies". In the drop-down menu select "Never ask" or "Ask for each site". Click "OK".

Mac Netscape 6.0

From the main menu at the top of the screen, choose "EDIT", then "PREFERENCES". Then choose "Advanced" and then "Cookies". Make sure "Enable all cookies" is checked. Click "OK".

Mac Netscape 7 or higher

From the main menu at the top of the screen, choose "EDIT", then "PREFERENCES". Then, under "Privacy & Security," select "Accept all cookies." Click "OK."

Mac Safari

From the main menu at the top of the screen, choose "SAFARI", then "PREFERENCES". Select "Security" and choose "Accept cookies only for sites you navigate to." Click "OK."

Mac Firefox

From the main menu at the top of the screen, choose "FIREFOX", then "PREFERENCES". Select "Privacy" and choose the "Allow sites to set cookies" option. Click "OK."

Mac Mozilla

From the main menu at the top of the screen choose {u201C}EDIT{u201D}, then {u201C}PREFERENCES{u201D}. Click {u201C}Privacy and Security,{u201D} and then click {u201C}Cookies.{u201D} Select {u201C}Allow all cookies{u201D} and {u201C}Accept cookies normally.{u201D}

Mac Opera

Go to www.NaxosMusicLibrary.com. From the main menu at the top of the screen choose {u201C}TOOLS{u201D}, then {u201C}ADVANCED{u201D}, then {u201C}COOKIES{u201D}. Make sure that {u201C}Cookies{u201D} is checked at the top of the new window. Select www.naxosmusiclibrary.com. Click {u201C}EDIT{u201D}. UNCHECK {u201C}Use defaults for normal cookies.{u201D} Check the four options below. Click {u201C}OK.{u201D} Click {u201C}Close.{u201D}

Wintel Internet Explorer 5.0 or higher

While you are online, go to your Internet "TOOLS" tab at the top of the page. Under the TOOLS tab, there is a tab that reads, "INTERNET OPTIONS". Click on this tab. Then, find the tab that reads, "PRIVACY" and click on it. Then, click on the "ADVANCED" tab. Here, you will select to "Override automatic cookie handling". You will also need to select to "Always allow cookies".

Wintel Netscape 7.0

From the main menu at the top of the screen, choose "EDIT", then "PREFERENCES". Then, under "Privacy & Security," select "Accept all cookies." Click "OK."

Wintel Netscape 8.0

From the main menu at the top of the screen, choose {u201C}TOOLS{u201D}, then {u201C}OPTIONS{u201D}. Then, under {u201C}Privacy,{u201D} select {u201C}Cookies.{u201D} From the drop-down menu, select to keep cookies {u201C}until they expire.{u201D}

Wintel Firefox

From the main menu at the top of the screen choose "TOOLS" and then "OPTIONS." Select "Privacy" and then "Cookies" and choose the "Allow sites to set cookies" option. Click "OK."

Wintel Mozilla

From the main menu at the top of the screen choose {u201C}EDIT{u201D}, then {u201C}PREFERENCES{u201D}. Click {u201C}Privacy and Security,{u201D} and then click {u201C}Cookies.{u201D} Select {u201C}Allow all cookies{u201D} and {u201C}Accept cookies normally.{u201D}

Wintel Opera

Make sure that {u201C}Cookies{u201D} is checked at the top of the new window. Select www.naxosmusiclibrary.com. Click {u201C}EDIT{u201D}.
