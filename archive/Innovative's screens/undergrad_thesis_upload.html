Thesis Submission Instructions

Is your thesis a word document or a PDF? If a word document, proceed to Step 1. If it's a PDF, proceed to Step 2.

Step 1: Upload your word document via the form below, then skip to Step 5:

Step 2: Go to Google Drive.

Step 3: Click the Upload button in the top left corner. Choose "Files...", navigate to your thesis file, and click Open.

Step 4: When the file is done uploading, click the "Share" button, and using the "Invite People" section, share with alexd@bennington.edu.

Step 5: Pat yourself on the back. You have officially submitted your thesis to Crossett Library!
