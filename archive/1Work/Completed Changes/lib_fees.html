Interlibrary Loan

Loan periods are determined by the lending library. The lending library may recall an item at any time. Renewals are at the discretion of the lending library. Photocopies do not need to be returned to the library.

Billing

Overdue Fines

Charges for accumulated fines are sent to the Business Office at the end of term. If you wish to avoid having fines appear on your student bill you may pay the fines at the library when you return the item. If you have questions or concerns regarding overdue fines please contact . In some circumstances the library may reduce or waive overdue fines, except for items on reserve or borrowed through interlibrary loan.

Replacement Fees

Charges for lost or damaged items are sent to the Business Office every month. If you wish to avoid having fines appear on your student bill you may pay the fee at the library. If you have questions or concerns regarding replacement fees please contact .

Interlibrary Loan

Replacement fees are determined by the lending library. Interlibrary loan overdue fines are $1.00 per item per day. If you have questions or concerns regarding interlibrary loan borrowing please contact . Because interlibrary loan items are the property of another institution, we can not reduce or waive overdue fines or replacement fees.

Laptops

Laptops that are returned late will be assessed overdue fines at a rate of $1.00 per hour. If a laptop computer is lost, stolen, or damaged, the borrower is responsible for repair or replacement cost as determined by library staff.
