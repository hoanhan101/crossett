Step 2: Thesis Submission Instructions

Is your thesis a word document or a PDF? If a word document, proceed to Step 3. If it's a PDF, proceed to Step 4.

Step 3: Upload your word document via the form below, then skip to Step 7:

Upload via Google Drive

Step 4: Go to Google Drive.

Step 5: Click the Upload button in the top left corner. Choose "Files...", navigate to your thesis file, and click Open.

Step 6: When the file is done uploading, click the "Share" button, and using the "Invite People" section, share with vhaver@bennington.edu.

Step 7: Pat yourself on the back. You have officially submitted your thesis to Crossett Library!
