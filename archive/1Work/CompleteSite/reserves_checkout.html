Can I Check Out Items on Reserve for My Own Class?

You may certainly check out items on reserve. The faculty default check-out period for reserve items is 7 days but if you need it longer just ask us to change the due date. It is a good idea to place items on reserve, such as films, if you know you will need to show them during a specific class date. If, after the showing, the item no longer needs to be on reserve, please tell Kathy Williams KathyW@bennington.edu x4605 and it will be put back in circulation.
