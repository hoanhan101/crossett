Reference Tip:

Envision world peace and the perfect reference source.

When deciding how to start your research, sometimes it is helpful to imagine what the perfect reference source would be. Is it a concise encyclopedia article or an expansive book? Is it quantitative or qualitative information that you want? Maybe both? Do you want primary or secondary sources? Is it a government study or a public opinion poll? Think about what kind of results you hope to obtain before you begin your search. You may end up saving a lot of time.
